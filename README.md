# TrueCal Notifier Privacy Policy

This repository only hosts the public privacy policy for TrueCal Notifier.

The policy is published in the eight app languages (English, Italian, French,
German, Spanish, Russian, Simplified Chinese and Japanese). Its commercial
wording is deliberate: an optional rewarded ad only enables a 72-hour
ad-free pass and never unlocks Pro functionality; permanent Pro is a separate
one-time Google Play purchase.

Privacy policy URL:

https://aferende.github.io/truecal-notifier-privacy/
